About Oorja Tech

Founded in 2010, Oorja Tech has emerged as a leading provider of electrical and automation solutions across India. With our headquarters in Pune, Maharashtra, we serve clients nationwide through our network of regional offices and service centers.

Our team of 200+ skilled engineers and technicians brings together decades of combined experience in industrial automation, electrical systems, and process control. We have successfully completed over 500 projects across various industries, establishing ourselves as a trusted partner for complex automation challenges.
